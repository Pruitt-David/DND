Name

Race
Certain Races have advantages over others, they determine your speed, and certain modifiers you gain on your abilities as well as languages.
Class
Certain classes have advantages over others, they determine your hit die, your feats, and your proficiencies in certain skills.
Level
Your level is a representation of how much experience your character has.
Alignment
Your character's alignment is a representation of how good / evil your character is, as well as how lawful / chaotic they are.

Abilities
Your abilities represent how effective your character is in certain things.
